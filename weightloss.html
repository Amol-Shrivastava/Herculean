WeightLoss at HERCULEAN

When it comes to losing weight, burning fat, and building muscle there are two styles of training we at HERCULEAN always recommend.
The first style of training We tell our trainee to begin with is Strength Training. It helps lower your body fat percentage and build lean muscle, and it can be modified for all levels. "The benefits of strength training for both men and women include an increase in bone mass and lean mass, improved body composition (due to decreased fat mass), cardiovascular fitness, strength, and an enhanced sense of well-being,"

The second style of training we recommend for fat loss is Cardio. We like cardio because it's been proven to help you burn fat, and it gives you variety in your workouts. You're probably either like, "Yes, I love running!" or "Nope, I'll pass." But here's the thing: running isn't the only form of cardio. You can also get your cardio in by doing workouts like indoor cycling and swimming. There's an option for everyone.

We at HERCULEAN work on extensive growth of our trainee's body and try to sculpt them in their desire shape.

HERCULEAN WeightLoss Programme for Beginners


For a newly added trainee in our gym, there are a few essential weightloss exercises on which we focus.

  • Wide grip pull-down

  • Tricep rope pushdown

  • Resistance band raise

  • EZ bar curl
