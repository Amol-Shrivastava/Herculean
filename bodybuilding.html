Body Building at HERCULEAN

“The majority of people think bodybuilders are ‘mass monsters’, and generally not very healthy-looking people,”as explained by IFBB pro champ and USN sports nutrition ambassador, Ryan Terry.
“But it’s really anyone who is trying to build or sculpt their body.” If you want to get serious about bodybuilding, you’ll need to navigate your nutrition and training with military precision.
Your regime will differ wildly if you’re gunning for gains (bulking) vs shredding fat (cutting) 12 weeks out from a competition. Needless to say, there’s not much room for a Friday night pint in either of these.

We at HERCULEAN work on extensive growth of our trainee's body and try to sculpt them in their desire shape.

HERCULEAN BodyBuilding for Beginners


For a newly added trainee in our gym, there are a few essential bodybuilding exercises on which we focus.

  • Wide grip pull-down

  • Tricep rope pushdown

  • Resistance band raise

  • EZ bar curl
