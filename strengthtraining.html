Strength Training at HERCULEAN

Strength training can be intimidating for beginners, but the benefits can’t be beaten: more muscle, higher calorie burn, stronger bones and joints, better endurance, and reduced risk of injury during another workout. We at HERCULEAN always pair it with cardio workout to give beginners a kick-start in their journey of being stronger.
Our Mantra for Strength Training is extremely simple-

“ We focus on mastering various movement patterns before adding an additional load, like dumbbells, for our beginners as it helps to decrease their risk of injury and helps them lift more weight down the road.”

There are five basic moves: squat, hinge, push, pull, and core work. There are many variations of each of those movements, but for beginners, I tend to gravitate toward a bodyweight squat, glute bridges, push-ups (on an incline if needed), inverted rows, and planks.
We practice five primary maneuvers, in addition to movements, such as throwing, crawling, and climbing:

  • Hip-dominant (deadlifts, hinges, and swings)
  • Knee-dominant (squats and lunges)
  • Pushing movements (pushups, dips, and presses)
  • Pulling movements (rows and pull-ups)
  • Gait patterns, such as walking and running

We at HERCULEAN work on extensive growth of our trainee's body and try to sculpt them in their desire shape.

HERCULEAN Strength Training Programme for Beginners


For a newly added trainee in our gym, there are a few essential strength training exercises on which we focus.

  • Wide grip pull-down

  • Tricep rope pushdown

  • Resistance band raise

  • EZ bar curl
