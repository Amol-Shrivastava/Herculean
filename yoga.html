Yoga Classes at HERCULEAN

Workout fads come and go, but virtually no other exercise program is as enduring as yoga. It's been around for more than 5,000 years. Yoga does more than burn calories and tone muscles. It's a total mind-body workout that combines strengthening and stretching poses with deep breathing and meditation or relaxation. There are more than 100 different forms of yoga. Some are fast-paced and intense. Others are gentle and relaxing. We at HERCULEAN always believed that yoga is eternal, the peace and harmony which comes after completing a yoga session is inexplicable.
The best quality of Yoga is you don't need any equipment other than a yoga mat as it relies on your body weight for resistance, and this the reason why yoga makes our body strong and flexible.
Yoga had always been associated with some breathing asans and mudras but never shown as something that strengthen our body and can make agile. Here are few of the many body parts that we strengthen in our yoga classes-

  • Cores
  • Arms
  • Legs
  • Glutes
  • Back

We at HERCULEAN work on extensive growth of our trainee's body and try to sculpt them in their desire shape.
